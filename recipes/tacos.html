Making Tacos

How to make authentic tacos

Ingredients

  • Meat of choice Steak, Chicken, or Pork.
  • Corn tortillas
  • Onions
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Cook you meat of choice.
  2. Dice the meat into small chunks.
  3. Heat tortillas to appropriate temperature.
  4. Dice oregano and onions into chunks.
  5. Add the choice of meat onto the tortilla, then add the oregano and onions.
