Making NY style Pizza

This simple pizza recipe only requires basic ingredients that can be found anywhere in the supermarket

Ingredients

  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Flour
  • Tomatoe Sause
  • Olive Oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Fill a bowl with flour and add water.
  2. Mix the flour and water to make dough.
  3. Knead the dough for about 5-10 minutes.
  4. On a flat surface strectch the dough into a circular shape.
  5. Add olive oil and tomatoe sauce on the sruface of the dough and spread evenly
  6. Add grated Mozzerella cheese to the surface.
  7. Cook in the oven for about 5-10 minutes
